About3/6/2015

Paul and Melissa Littleton are passionate fitness professionals who want to help you reach and maintain your fitness goals. THey bring a well-rounded breadth of knowledge to Fitness 360, offering boxing, kickboxing, yoga, weight training, nutritional, and spiritual wellness services.

The couple got married in 2018 during a 200hr yoga teacher training program they decided to embark on together. The bond they share in fitness and nutrition strengthens their union everyday and they want to share their love for fitness with you.

If you'd like more information on our services, register for an account or send us an email. We can't wait to hear from you!
